How will I be judged?

You will be judged by the merit of your existing work and resume as well as your research proposal and project timeline.

Will I be considered for grant amounts other than what I apply for?

Yes - if we feel your research proposal warrants more funding than you request, we may issue a larger grant amount. If we really like your proposal but feel it does not warrant the requested grant amount, we may offer a lower amount.

What happens after I submit my application?

Once the application period closes, we will work with a team to judge each applicant fairly. We will follow up directly with questions and follow-up interviews.

When will the next application window open?

The plan is to accept new applications every six months. After the initial window, the next application window will open in August 2018.
